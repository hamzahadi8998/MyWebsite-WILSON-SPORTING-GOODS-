About Us

The World SPORTS Organisation was established in 2007 by Founder and CEO Scott Gray. Our purpose is to raise the level of conversation around Sports worldwide, through a year-round programme of exhibitions, awards and competitions.Our mission is to support sports goods around the world, to celebrate and show their work, as well as nurture the medium of sports products on a global scale. Our value lies in the integrity of our expertise, our years of experience and the strength of our relationships with both individual products as well as our industry-leading partners around the world.
